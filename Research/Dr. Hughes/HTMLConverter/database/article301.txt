               Mathematician's Dice Allow You to Roll π

   For the nerd in your life, or the nerd in your mirror, Matt
   Chisholm is funding the production of these awesome
   Mathematicians Dice through Kickstarter, the crowdsourcing
   fundraising site. The dice feature the imaginary number i, the
   golden ratio φ and π, among others.
